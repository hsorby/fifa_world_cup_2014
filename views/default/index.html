The Competition

The aim of this competition is to accurately predict the outcome of all the games in the Football World Cup. Points are scored for correct predictions and the person with the most points at end of the tournament will take home a massive eye-boggling prize of some as-yet-undetermined monetary value.

Predictions are entered via this website under the Pool Games and Knockout Games pages. All predictions that are entered before the kickoff time for that match will be considered with the last prediction made before kickoff used to score points.

To make things just a little bit interesting we (the royal we) decided to set an entry fee of $5 and the winner will take home 60% of the total prize pool. We also decided that some spot prizes would be a good idea and discussions led to setting a spot prize of 15% which will be awarded at the end of pool play and a further spot prize of 25% which will be awarded at the end of the tournament to an entrant who has not collected the main prize.
